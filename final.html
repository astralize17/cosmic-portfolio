Sustainability

Our project uses a repurposed jumpsuit from the closet of a team member, and upcycling various fabric scraps. Our peice represents the future of a non renewable path for mankind and its ramifications.

Planning Process

This group was inspired by constellations and space intially when presented this futuristic idea. Though many iterations we toyed with the idea of spaceships, dune, exterterrestrial life, and managed between all of us to land on a combination of all of the above more of less.

Creation Process

The creation process taught us not just about the intersection between technology and fashion, but also about working in a group with your freinds, and the importance of communication, and collaboration.

Final Design

The final peice is a wonderful culmination of ideas that we pondered, and we hope you will enjoy it as much as we do!
